Tomcat 9 upgrade

After executing the following Kubernetes Deployment (Rolling Update) Tomcat 8 will be updated to v9.0.52

$ ​kubectl apply -f tc9-deploy-v2.yaml --record
After executing the above command both Tomcat 8 and 9 will point towards Tomcat 9 (9.0.52)
